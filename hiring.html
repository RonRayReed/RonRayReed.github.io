The Opportunity

Reed Intelligence Group is expanding its city desk operations. We are looking for Business Analysts to cover capital markets, reconstruction finance, EU accession, and business developments in Kyiv, Lviv, Odesa, and Chișinău.

This is a reporting role that requires both financial analysis capability and editorial judgment. You will research, verify, and write business intelligence in the Reed Intel editorial voice: short declarative sentences, no superfluous adjectives, every factual claim linked to a named source.

The Role

  • Monitor government economic data releases, chamber of commerce bulletins, and IFI announcements daily
  • Write 3–5 sourced business intelligence stories per week in Reed Intel editorial style
  • Maintain and expand the 40 Notable directory for your city
  • Translate and summarize relevant Ukrainian or Romanian-language source material
  • Contribute to the weekly Reed Intel regional digest

What We Look For

  • Professional-level English writing — our product is English-language business intelligence
  • Ukrainian (for Kyiv/Lviv/Odesa positions) or Romanian (for Chișinău position) reading proficiency
  • Understanding of financial statements, IFI program structures, or capital markets basics
  • Familiarity with primary source verification — government databases, corporate filings, court records
  • Located in or willing to relocate to the relevant city
