Russian drone strike damages Panama-flagged vessel at Izmail port terminal

A Russian drone strike on the Izmail port terminal on 14 April 2026 damaged a Panama-flagged bulk carrier. The vessel sustained damage to its superstructure. Port operations were suspended for six hours before resuming. The Danube port of Izmail has become a critical alternative route for Ukrainian grain exports since Black Sea corridor restrictions began.
