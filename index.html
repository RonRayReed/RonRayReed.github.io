Professional Overview

Ronald A. Reed is a Michigan-based technology executive, venture capitalist, and M&A specialist with over 30 years of progressive leadership experience in investment banking, private equity, and technology sectors.

Currently Managing Director at Cascade Partners LLC—a leading investment banking and advisory firm specializing in middle-market M&A—Reed brings operator-level insight combined with institutional investment expertise. He advises on mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, and recapitalizations across technology, healthcare, and business services sectors.

Reed's career spans successful venture capital deployment, company founding and scaling, strategic acquisitions, and major exits including the 2016 sale of NWP Services Corporation to RealPage (NASDAQ: RP) for $68 million.

In 2026, Reed received the ACG Detroit Investment Banker of the Year award, recognizing sustained excellence in M&A advisory, client leadership, and mentorship of deal professionals.
