About PIX

Personal Injury Exchange, or PIX, is a developing data and market infrastructure platform for the personal injury ecosystem. PIX is intended to operate as an independent trusted third party that helps law firms, healthcare providers, lien buyers, and related participants use better information to make better decisions.

The goal is not simply to create another directory or software tool. PIX is being developed to help the market become more transparent, more orderly, more compliant, and more efficient.

Executive summary PIX will provide a trusted platform for collecting, organizing, benchmarking, and reporting personal injury market data. By enabling participants to contribute structured invoice, lien, settlement, and claims information, PIX will help transform fragmented market knowledge into practical benchmarks.
Why the market needs it Personal injury law firms, healthcare providers, and lien buyers often operate with limited visibility into comparable claims, expected settlement ranges, lien recovery patterns, provider performance, insurer behavior, and case-level economics. PIX is designed to help reduce this information gap.
How participants benefit Lawyers can gain better market context for claims and provider relationships. Healthcare providers can improve visibility, referrals, cash-flow options, and benchmarking. Lien buyers can improve sourcing, diligence, pricing, and portfolio analysis.
Long-term vision PIX is expected to evolve from directory and data contribution workflows into a broader market platform for benchmarking, reporting, rapid onboarding, case tracking, and trusted market intelligence.
