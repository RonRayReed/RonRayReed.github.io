Reed Intelligence Group

Business intelligence for investors, executives, and policy professionals in Emerging Europe.

Our Mission

Reed Intelligence Group produces English-language business intelligence for Kyiv, Lviv, Odesa, and Chișinău. Our readers are executives, investors, policy professionals, and analysts who need accurate, sourced, and timely market information.

We do not editorialize. We do not speculate. Every factual claim links to a named source. Every story earns its space.

Our target audiences span the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Germany, and in-country readers who need English-language coverage of markets where local-language sources dominate.

Our Editorial Lens

Every city. Every issue. The same six questions.

01 Who moved money?
Capital flows, deal announcements, IFI disbursements.
02 Who changed the rules?
Legislative, regulatory, or policy changes with market impact.
03 What did the numbers say?
Government data releases, sector reports, IFI projections.
04 Who entered or exited?
Market entries, exits, restructurings, new licenses.
05 What is the accession score?
EU integration progress for Ukraine and Moldova.
06 What is the security impact?
Conflict-related events with direct business consequence.

Editorial Standards

Source attribution
Every factual claim in every story links to a named primary source. No anonymous sourcing without compelling editorial justification.
No editorializing
Reed Intel reports facts and data. Opinion is clearly labeled. Analysis is framed as analysis. News is framed as news.
No paid placement
Companies and institutions cannot pay for editorial coverage. The 40 Notable lists are editorially determined and not for sale.
Corrections policy
Factual errors are corrected promptly with a note appended to the article. Corrections are not hidden.
