Purcari Q1 2026 export revenue exceeds 2025 full-year pace

Purcari Wineries, Moldova's BSE-listed premium wine producer, reports Q1 2026 EU export revenue that already exceeds its full-year 2024 revenue total — driven by new German and French distribution agreements.

Reed Intel DeskChisinau Business Intel
PublishedApril 2026
Read time4 min

Purcari Wineries, the Chișinău-based premium wine producer listed on the Bucharest Stock Exchange, reported Q1 2026 EU export revenue that exceeded the total EU export revenue it recorded for the full calendar year 2024. The company attributed the result to new distribution agreements signed in Germany and France in late 2025 and to volume growth in its existing Polish and Czech Republic markets.

Purcari's flagship Negru de Purcari and Roșu de Purcari wines received gold medals at the Decanter World Wine Awards 2025, providing a marketing catalyst for negotiations with German and French off-trade retailers. The company's Q1 2026 investor update confirmed that both distribution agreements were generating initial order volumes above the company's internal projections.

BSE listing context

Purcari is listed on the Bucharest Stock Exchange, making it one of the few Moldovan companies with a regulated market listing. The company has discussed the possibility of a dual listing on a Western European exchange but has not made a formal announcement.
