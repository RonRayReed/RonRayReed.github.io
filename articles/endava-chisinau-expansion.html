Endava Chișinău expands engineering center by 300 engineers

Endava, the NYSE-listed IT services firm and Moldova's largest private IT employer, is expanding its Chișinău engineering center by 300 engineers, bringing total Chișinău headcount to approximately 2,100.

Reed Intel DeskChisinau Business Intel
PublishedMarch 2026
Read time4 min

Endava, the London-headquartered and NYSE-listed digital services firm, announced an expansion of its Chișinău engineering center in March 2026. The expansion adds 300 engineering positions, bringing total Chișinău headcount to approximately 2,100 staff — a 17% increase. Endava is Moldova's largest private sector IT employer and operates IT Park Moldova membership status.

The expansion is driven by new client mandates in the financial services and healthcare technology sectors. Endava CEO John Cotterell noted in the company's investor communications that Chișinău offers favorable cost structures relative to Western European engineering hubs, strong university talent pipelines, and improving infrastructure as a result of EU accession alignment.
